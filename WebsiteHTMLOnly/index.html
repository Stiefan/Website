Rapid Prototyping

Facilitating ideation workshops for products and business models

About


We provide workshops for entrepreneurs and companies in their start-up phase with as starting point the challenge or vision of this company. The goal of the workshops are not to get from product to launch in 2 hours, but to create the "prototype" mindset.

During the workshops we use Rapid Prototyping to move in small steps from target group, to minimum viable product, measuring impact and finally to pivot or optimize. With this rapid prototyping session we (a) present a tool to (b) create a mindset that every new idea, every new assumption should be tested in an early stage.
